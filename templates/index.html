What is ATS and Why It Matters

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software used by employers to manage job applications. They scan resumes for keywords and format compatibility before a human sees them.

Benefits of ATS Optimization:
  • Increase Visibility: Get past the initial filters to reach recruiters.
  • Save Time: Tailor your resume quickly with keyword insights.
  • Boost Chances: Higher scores mean more interview opportunities.
  • Modern Edge: Stay competitive in a digital hiring world.
Quick Tips for Better Scores:
  • Use standard fonts and no images/tables.
  • Include job-specific keywords.
  • Keep sections clear: Summary, Experience, Skills, Education.
  • Save as PDF for consistent parsing.
